組合

如何開啟組合

  • To create additional Assemblies, use the plus sign menu  at the bottom of the window and select Create Assembly.

要創建其他裝配體，使用底部的加號選單，然後選擇“創建裝配體”。

  • Insert Parts and Assemblies 插入零件與組合件

  • Assembly List 

  • Standard Content 標準零件

  1. Click the Standard content filter on the Insert parts and assemblies dialog.
  2. Select the specifics about the content you want to insert: Standard, Category, Class, and Component.
  3. Select  (the auto-size icon) and then a hole on the model to automatically select the proper size in the dialog.

    Or, select the size manually from the dropdown.

    If there is no standard part that matches the size of the hole selected, a message is displayed:

  4. The part number and description, if present in the system, are automatically supplied. If you wish, click Edit to activate the Part number and Description fields and type metadata regarding the standard content part. This information will then be available for that part for all users of the Onshape account.

    This field is company-wide if you are part of a company, or limited to your user account if you are not part of a company.

    A preview of the selected part is shown.

  5. Click Insert to automatically insert the part if a planar or cylindrical face or an edge is already selected. If no selection is made, the part appears at your mouse location: drag it to the preferred location and click to snap to a Mate connector.

    Use the Insert closest to selection and Insert furthest from selection icons when inserting subsequent standard content to automatically place the content in the stack of content mated to the selected part. To flip the orientation of the part being inserted, press the 'a' key on your keyboard.

    In the example below, Insert closest to selection will insert at the shorter (leftmost) arrow and Insert furthest from selection will insert at the longer (rightmost) arrow:

  6. Click  on the dialog to save your actions and create instances of the standard content in the assembly and list it in the Instances lists (along with the mate connector).

Mate Connector (ctrl+m) 結合連接器

在零件、曲面或草圖建立結合連接器。

Fastened Mate (m) 緊固結合

移除兩個零件間的所有自由度。

Revolute Mate (m) 轉動結合

在組合的兩個零件間建立轉動結合的限制條件。

零件僅能繞著軸旋轉。

Slider Mate (m) 滑動結合

在組合的兩個零件間建立滑動結合的限制條件。

零件僅能沿著軸平移。

Planar Mate (m) 平面結合

在組合的兩個零件間建平面結合的限制條件。

零件可以在平面平移。

Cylindrical Mate (m) 圓柱結合

在組合的兩個零件間建立圓柱結合的限制條件。

零件可以在繞著軸旋轉平移。

Pin Slot Mate (m) 銷槽結合

在組合的兩個零件間建立銷槽結合的限制條件。

零件可以沿著槽的中心路徑平移。

Ball Mate (m) 球體結合

在組合的兩個零件間建立球體結合的限制條件。

零件可以在所的三個軸上旋轉。

Parallel Mate (m) 平行結合

在組合的兩個零件間建立平行結合的限制條件。

零件可以相對地自由平移與平行旋轉。

Tangent Mate (m) 相切結合

在組合的兩個零件間建立相切/凸輪/路徑結合。

零件移動時，零件的相切關係會維持住。
