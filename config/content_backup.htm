介面：

The Feature List : Features are used to create CAD geometry and this list shows the order in which they were created. (Scroll down in the left panel to see them all.)

The Part List : This is where parts are listed, in chronological order, as they are created.

Tabs: Documents can have multiple tabs such as Part Studios, Assemblies, Drawings, and more. More on this later.

The Graphics Area : Where we view, create, and modify geometry.

The View Cube : This helps us rotate and view our model.
